Microsoft Office is one of the most commonly used productivity tools in the business world. It is a very essential and useful skills to get employment in organisations. This Microsoft Office Course covered different features of the main Microsoft Office productivity tools such as Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel and Microsoft PowerPoint that can gives students an in-depth understanding.

After completing this course, you will able to:
  • Use Different Features of Word, Excel and PowerPoint
  • Gain a Better Understanding of Microsoft Office Productivity Tools
  • Boost your Productivity and Confidence to Apply the Microsoft Tools
